The Flyte Hoverboard is a real-life skateboard that hovers in the air via magnetic levitation. It's not a functional transportation device, but rather a symbol of the future, a dream that comes true. The board represents the freedom and limitless possibilities that come with defying gravity. The piece is not meant to be ridden, but to be admired and experienced as a work of art.

The process of creating the Flyte Hoverboard was a long and challenging journey. I spent 15 years researching and experimenting with electromagnetic levitation, and the final product is a testament to my dedication and perseverance.

With your support, we can all enjoy the beauty and magic of a hoverboard. Flyte is a real-life art piece with a message: to awaken the childlike artist within us, to remind us that the future is now.
